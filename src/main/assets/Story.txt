The stars sparkled like diamonds in the black velvet sky. Astronaut Sarah gazed out of the window of the space station, her breath misting up the glass. She had always dreamed of going to space, and now here she was, floating weightlessly above the Earth. Suddenly, a red light started flashing, and an alarm sounded. Sarah's heart raced as she checked the monitor. There was a breach in the hull! She raced to grab her emergency kit, her training kicking in as she quickly patched up the hole. As she caught her breath, Sarah looked out at the endless expanse of space. She felt humbled and awed by the vastness and mystery of it all. She knew that there was still so much more to discover out there, and she was determined to explore it all.
